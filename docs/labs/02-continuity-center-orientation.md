# Lab 02: Continuity Center Orientation

## Objective

Use Azure Continuity Center as the primary operational dashboard.

## Steps

1. Open Azure Continuity Center in the portal.
2. Filter by the lab resource group.
3. Review protected assets and health indicators.
4. Identify any assets with incomplete protection.
5. Navigate to linked Recovery Services Vault views from Continuity Center.

## Expected Outcome

You can locate the lab workload, identify current protection posture, and pivot into detailed ASR views.
